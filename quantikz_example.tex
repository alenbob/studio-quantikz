\section*{Quantikz Stress Examples}

This file is a stress corpus for the editor rather than a minimal tutorial.
Each circuit targets a feature or edge case that the app currently imports and exports.

\subsection*{1. Baseline single-qubit path}
\[
\begin{quantikz}
\lstick{\ket{0}} & \gate{H} & \gate{Z} & \meter{}
\end{quantikz}
\]
